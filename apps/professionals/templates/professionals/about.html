Building Kenya's Future,

We bridge the gap between clients and qualified building professionals — making quality construction accessible, transparent, and trustworthy across East Africa.

Our Mission

Connecting Expertise with Ambition

Founded in Nairobi, BuildConnect was born from a simple frustration: finding a reliable, qualified construction professional in Kenya was unnecessarily difficult. Clients struggled to verify credentials, compare expertise, and connect with trustworthy experts.

We built the platform we wished existed — a curated marketplace where every professional is vetted, every review is genuine, and every connection leads to quality work.

Today, we serve clients across Kenya's major cities, helping them bring homes, offices, gardens, and infrastructure projects to life with confidence.
